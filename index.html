About Kamungor Homes

Building affordable, quality homes across Africa since 2018. Making the dream of home ownership accessible to every family.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, quality housing solutions that empower African families to build wealth through home ownership while creating sustainable communities.

We believe that everyone deserves a safe, comfortable home. Our innovative approach combines traditional African architectural wisdom with modern construction techniques to create homes that are both affordable and built to last.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading provider of affordable housing, having built 10,000 homes across the continent while maintaining our commitment to sustainability and community development.

  • 10,000 homes across Africa
  • 25,000 families housed
  • Carbon-neutral construction
  • 100% renewable energy communities
